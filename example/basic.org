* Setup

Before running examples, load ob-gleam:

#+begin_src emacs-lisp :results silent
(add-to-list 'load-path (expand-file-name ".."))
(require 'ob-gleam)
(org-babel-do-load-languages
 'org-babel-load-languages
 '((gleam . t)))
#+end_src

* Hello World

Simple expression auto-wrapped in =pub fn main()= with =import gleam/io=:

#+begin_src gleam
  io.println("Hello, world!")
#+end_src

* Arithmetic

#+begin_src gleam :imports gleam/int
  io.println(int.to_string(1 + 2))
#+end_src
